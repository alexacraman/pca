How to join

PCA membership for professionals requires an initial fee of £35 and annual renewal cost for continued membership of £30 each year. This can be paid via cheque or bank transfer using the details given on completion of this application.

Free PCA membership for students currently studying Conductive Education.

Subscription period is from 1st November to 31st October each year.
