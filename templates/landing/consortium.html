CE Consortium UK

The CE Consortium was established in 2016 with the purpose of bringing together senior leaders in the field of CE across the UK. The main aim was to provide a forum where CE providers could work together on common issues, discuss challenges and solutions and share good practice.

The group is open to any senior leader working in the field of CE who wishes to be a part of the national CE agenda.
